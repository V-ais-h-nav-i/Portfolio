Connect - Music Streaming Web App

Connect

Connect is a music streaming web application that allows users to enjoy the best of NCS (No Copyright Sounds) tracks. The app features a user-friendly interface with a list of curated songs, each accompanied by its cover image and duration. Users can easily play, pause, and skip tracks using intuitive playback controls. The app also includes a progress bar that lets users seek through the track duration.
  • Curated Song List: The app presents a handpicked selection of NCS tracks, each with a thumbnail and duration for easy navigation.
  • Play and Pause Controls: Users can play and pause songs with a simple click on the respective track's play button or using the master play control at the bottom.
  • Track Seek: A progress bar allows users to seek to specific points in a song's timeline, enabling them to jump to their favorite sections effortlessly.
  • Smooth User Experience: The web app provides a seamless and enjoyable listening experience with smooth audio playback and responsive controls.
Technologies Used: Front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), Icon Library (Font Awesome), Audio Handling (HTML5 Audio API)
